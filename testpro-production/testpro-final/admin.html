Step-by-Step: Deploy on Netlify + testproindia.com

1
Get your domain
Purchase testproindia.com from GoDaddy, Namecheap, or BigRock (India). Cost: ~₹800-1500/year. If already purchased, skip to step 2.
2
Download your site files
Download the TestPro zip (your current project). Make sure it includes index.html, admin.html, shared.css, and all article/page files.
3
Deploy to Netlify
Go to netlify.com → Sign up free → Click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually" → Drag and drop your site folder. Your site will be live at a random Netlify URL instantly.
4
Connect testproindia.com
In Netlify → Site settings → Domain management → Add custom domain → Enter testproindia.com. Netlify will give you nameservers (e.g. dns1.p01.nsone.net).
5
Update DNS nameservers
In your domain registrar (GoDaddy/Namecheap), go to DNS settings → Replace nameservers with the ones Netlify provided. DNS propagation takes 1-24 hours.
6
Enable free SSL (HTTPS)
Netlify auto-generates a Let's Encrypt SSL certificate once DNS propagates. Enable it in Site settings → HTTPS → Verify DNS configuration → Provision certificate.
7
Update content & redeploy
Whenever you add new articles or content via this admin panel, download the updated files and drag-drop again to Netlify. Or connect a GitHub repo for automatic deploys.
💡 Pro Tip — Use Google AdSense
Once your site is live at testproindia.com, apply for Google AdSense to monetize your traffic. Your site already has ad slot placeholders built in. Apply at adsense.google.com after getting 20-30 articles published.

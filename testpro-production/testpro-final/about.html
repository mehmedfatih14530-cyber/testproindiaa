About TestPro India

Built by QA engineers, for QA engineers. We know exactly what Indian testers need to grow their careers — because we've been there.

Why We Built This

When we started our careers as software testers in Pune, we noticed a big gap: there were plenty of global QA resources, but almost nothing tailored to the Indian IT market — nothing that talked about cracking Infosys interviews, navigating ISTQB registration in India, or understanding realistic salary expectations in Bangalore vs Hyderabad.

TestPro India exists to fill that gap. Every article, every interview question, and every salary guide on this site is written with the Indian QA professional in mind — freshers joining service companies, mid-level testers transitioning to automation, and senior QAs eyeing leadership roles.

Our Mission

To make every software tester in India — regardless of college tier, city, or background — capable of building a high-earning, respected career in quality assurance and test automation.

🎯 Practical First

No fluff. Every piece of content must answer "how do I actually use this at work?"

🇮🇳 India-Focused

Company names, salary data, and exam tips are all specific to the Indian IT market.

🆓 Always Free to Read

Core content will always be free. We earn through ads and optional premium courses.

✅ Verified & Tested

All code samples are tested before publishing. We don't publish what we haven't verified.

Who We Are

TestPro India is run by working QA professionals based in Pune, Maharashtra. Our team has experience at Indian IT services companies (TCS, Infosys, Wipro) as well as product companies. We write in our spare time because we genuinely care about the QA community in India.
